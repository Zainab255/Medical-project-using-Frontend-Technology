MEET OUR NURSES

Nurses are healthcare professionals who work closely with doctors, patients, and other
healthcare team members to provide care, administer medications, and monitor patients'
conditions. They play a vital role in promoting health, preventing illness, and providing
support during recovery.

NURSE AMNA

Direct Patient Care: Nurses provide hands-on care to patients, including administering medications, dressing wounds,providing comfort, and improving overall quality of life.

NURSE MINAHIL

Assessment and Monitoring: Nurses assess patients' physical and emotional conditions, noting any changes in symptoms or health status.providing comfort, and improving overall quality of life.

NURSE ALISHA

Patient Education: Nurses play a key role in educating patients and their families about medical conditions, treatment plans, and self-care practices. providing comfort, and improving overall quality of life.

NURSE KHADIJHA

Medication Administration: Nurses administer medications as prescribed by physicians, ensuring proper dosages, routes, and times. providing comfort, and improving overall quality of life.
